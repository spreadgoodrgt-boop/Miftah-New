Welcome to Miftah

A different way to
engage with the Quran

Miftah is a study companion for intermediate Arabic students who want to go beyond translation — into grammar, tafsir, personal reflection, and memorisation. Built around the methodology of Ustadh Nouman Ali Khan's 5 Lenses framework from Bayyinah.

The Heart Filter
"All study passes through a filter that makes it meaningful or renders it useless. That filter is the heart. Our tadabbur proceeds from faith and readiness to receive guidance."

— Sohaib Saeed, Bayyinah
The Methodology

5 Lenses to reflect
upon the Quran

Each ayah can be explored through five distinct lenses. One tab, one focus at a time.

Lens 1
Wording
Grammar, morphology, i'rab, and why Allah chose this word here.
Lens 2
World of the Quran
Context of revelation, history, and the world of creation.
Lens 3
My Experience
Personal reflection — how the Quran holds a mirror to you.
Lens 4
Connections
Related ayat, surah themes, hadith and seerah connections.
Lens 5
General Lessons
The universal principle — the fact of life Allah is teaching you here.
